Invalidity & Patentability

Freedom to Operate (FTO)

Risk Analysis & Clearance

Before launching a product or entering a market, understanding your freedom to operate is critical. Aube Research conducts systematic FTO searches to identify active patents that may cover your product, technology, or process — across all relevant jurisdictions.

Our reports include validity assessments, claim mapping against your product features, and clear risk tiering, giving legal and business teams what they need to make informed go/no-go decisions.

Product Clearance Multi-Jurisdiction Risk Tiering Validity Assessment

Patent landscapes deliver a comprehensive picture of who holds what in a technology space — who the key players are, where the white spaces lie, and how the IP landscape is evolving over time. Aube Research's landscapes are data-rich, visually clear, and strategically oriented.

Portfolio Analysis

Strength & Monetization

Aube Research evaluates patent portfolios along multiple dimensions: claim scope and strength, forward/backward citation networks, prosecution quality, geographic coverage, and commercial relevance. Essentiality & Licensing

We evaluate declared patents against the relevant standard specification sections to determine technical essentiality, supporting licensing negotiations, FRAND rate-setting, and litigation defense.

Our Principles

Three Words We Live By

Precision.

Every claim mapping, every search query, every deliverable is held to a standard of technical accuracy that legal and commercial teams can rely on without reservation. Precision is not a feature — it is the baseline.

Depth.

Patent intelligence at the surface level is not intelligence at all. Aube Research goes beyond keyword search and database retrieval to deliver insight grounded in technical understanding, prior art context, and claim construction expertise.

Judgment.

AI can identify patterns. Only experienced analysts can exercise judgment. Aube Research's team brings the legal reasoning, technical domain knowledge, and contextual understanding that transform data into actionable intelligence.

Mission

Aube Research exists to give IP teams, law firms, and technology companies the analytical depth they need to make high-stakes patent decisions with clarity and confidence.

Methodology

How We Work

Aube Research's methodology is built on a structured integration of AI-assisted workflows and human expert oversight. We do not treat AI as a shortcut — we treat it as infrastructure that our analysts operate and validate.

This approach allows us to handle volume and complexity that would be impractical with purely manual methods, while maintaining the quality and defensibility that our clients require.

Confidentiality is embedded into every layer of our operation. Client data, project scope, and deliverables are handled under strict information security protocols and NDAs as standard practice.
